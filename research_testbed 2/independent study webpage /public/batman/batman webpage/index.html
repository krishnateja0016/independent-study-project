BATMAN

THE DARK KNIGHT

Origin Story

Bruce Wayne, a billionaire philanthropist, vowed to protect Gotham City after witnessing his parents’ tragic murder. Through years of training and mastery of fear, he became the vigilante known as Batman—the Dark Knight who embodies justice.
